About HOPE CC's history





About a decade ago, a couple devoted to serve

the Lord decided to go back to their homeland.

They thought of planting a church in the heart

of the Philippine Islands where their roots are from.

The Panay mission humbly started from a small

home Bible study in Savannah Iloilo Subdivision.

Around January 2014, their hearts’ desire was partially

answered. With God’s abundant provision, a 566 sqm lot

located in the Municipality of Pavia in Iloilo Province was

acquired.

Pastor Godfrey and his wife, Gloria, then decided to build the new

church in this locality. They foresaw that many families would be

brought closer to the Lord with the more than twenty new villages

and subdivisions being developed around the locality.

God’s ministry continued as a church building was designed and

constructed with the help of Pastor Godfrey’s nephew who was an architect.

The design was not only a place of worship but also incorporated a library,

an AV room, a pastor’s office, a small room for the Children’s ministry and

spacious rooms to accommodate visiting ministers and their families.

The construction started on September 2015 and was completed by October 1, 2016.



2016 Milestones - The CHURCH BUILDING was completed!

A small group started to gather in May and by July of 2016, worship services have begun.

Indeed, the Lord brought some 20 families who regularly attend these church services.

By God’s overwhelming grace, on October 1, 2016, a time capsule was placed as a symbol of Hope

Christian Church of Pavia, Inc humble beginnings. Thus, the first formal

worship service was held on October 2, 2016,

which also marks the October Anniversary month.

As a formal organization, it was duly registered under the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)

as a non-profit non-stock organization by November 22, 2017.
